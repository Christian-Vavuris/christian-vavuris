What is a Digital Letter Of Marque?

How do they work?

The author of a new Letter of Marque provides all necessary information via this questionnaire. Once reviewed to ensure the request isn't illegal, both a crypto wallet and subreddit are created for the specific Letter of Marque. The author then funds the wallet with the initial prize amount, and from there the letter is published so that the internet can act upon it.

Evidence of Marque completion is subsequently published in the subreddit, where prizes are awarded based on the terms of each Letter.

Can I contribute to a Marque?

Yes! Financial contributions to each Marque can be sent to the digital asset wallet listed in each letter. 10% of each donation goes toward site and team maintenance, while the remaining 90% goes toward the prize.

Informational contributions can be provided in the associated subreddit.

What if there is a dispute over a prize?

Our goal is to fairly compensate Marque participants based on the specific terms of each letter. The Administrator General serves as final adjudicator of any and all disputes, and may decide on alternate prize terms if they are justified.
