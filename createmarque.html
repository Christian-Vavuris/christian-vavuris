Steps to Create a Letter of Marque

  1. Fill out this questionnaire with information on your Lettter, its purpose, and the associated prize.
  2. The Marque team conducts a quick review and responds if there are questions.
  3. A draft Letter of Marque is sent for you to review. It includes the letter's associated subreddit and crypto wallet.
  4. You fund the wallet with the initial prize amount (Marque takes a fee.)
  5. Once funded your letter is published and can be shared with anyone who might be able to solve it.
  6. Submissions to the subreddit are montitored, and prizes are issued for winning posts.
