On Rocket, the Dog that Walked on the Moon

Last week NASA revealed there was a secret 4th member of the first trip to the moon - a golden retriever named Rocket. At the same meeting NASA executives hinted that photographs of Rocket in his space suit may exist, and that he was a very good boy. This letter of Marque aims to incentivize the publication of said pictures of Rocket, so that the public may see that he was, indeed, a good boy.

Issued By:

Anonymous Association of Rocket Fans

Terms of the Marque

This marque shall be won by the first individual to share a picture of Rocket in the associated subreddit. 100% of the cryptocurrency prize shall be paid to the first person who shares the photograph of Rocket in the associated subreddit.

Authenticity shall be determined via confirmation by NASA, or via the discretion of the Author & Administrator General.
